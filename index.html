Swimming Lessons

Children under 7 years must be dropped off and picked up from the pool deck by a parent or guardian.

Parent & Tot (6 to 36 months)

Introduce your child to the wonders of water through a fun and interactive lesson experience. Join one of our experienced instructors for games, water activities and an introduction to swimming skills. Swimming lessons can improve comfort in the water. Parent participation in the water is required.

Preschool (3 to 5 years)

This 7-level program is designed to promote success, focusing on parent involvement and water safety. It has an exciting and attractive format with fun characters and activities geared towards those 3 to 5 years old. Once your little one turns 6, they will move seamlessly into the Swim Kids program.

Red Cross Swim Kids (6 to 12 years)

This 10-level program focuses on stroke development, water safety and includes a fitness component. With our Quadrant program, your kids will be challenged to their individual level and learning style.

Adults

Our adult swimming lessons are for beginners and experienced swimmers. We adapt the program to suit all participants. We also offer women only swimming lessons.

Private Swim Lessons

Private swim lessons are available at all our Indoor Pools. Call us for more details

The City Of Ottawa Is Committed To Helping Children Swim Safely

Drowning is the second leading cause of preventable death for children under 7. In Surrey, our goal is to expose children to the water in a safe and fun environment. Through safety knowledge and experience, we can help ensure your children have a safe and enjoyable swim.

Working in partnership with the Lifesaving Society of Ottawa helps kids improve their comfort in the water. Swimming lessons provide skills for safety. Through our registered and drop-in lessons, our instructors can help.

Open water safety

Swimming in open water creates challenges unique to the outdoor setting. See information about our community pool swimming area for quick tips on indoor water safety.
